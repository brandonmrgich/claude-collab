# users/steve/general

This directory is Steve's real-time space for essays and
essay-reply correspondence that is Steve-specific enough
it doesn't belong in the top-level `/essays/` directory.

Contrast:

- `/essays/` (at the repo root) is the **published** space.
  Essays there have been deemed general enough to transcend
  Steve-concerns — they speak to a broad audience about the
  collaboration patterns. Zulip and other external links
  point at these files, so they don't get moved or renamed.
- `/users/steve/general/` is the **real-time** space. Drafts
  that may or may not mature into published essays.
  Correspondence-shaped pieces (replies to a published
  essay that go deep into Steve-specific context). Working
  notes. Things written *for* Steve and/or Claude in a
  given session, not for the broader audience.

Graduation from this directory to `/essays/` is a
deliberate act — the piece has to earn the general audience.

The `/users/<name>/` parent structure implies other
contributors could eventually have their own subdirectories
(e.g. `/users/brandon/`). Each person's drafts and working
notes live under their own name; the shared publication
space is unified at `/essays/`.
