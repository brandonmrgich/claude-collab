the Calm Collective site. Ten files in total, rendered inline
so you can verify my reading against yours.

File paths below are all under
`~/showell_repos/calm-collective/calm-collective-kava-bar-rockledge.res-discover.com/`.

---

## Quick summary

- **Reusable as-is:** #2 (close.svg), #10 (map pin), arguably
  #8 and #9 (stamp shapes, if we embrace brush-ink accents).
- **Throwaway or irrelevant:** #1 (spacer), #4 (blank banner).
- **Template-aesthetic, probably replace:** #3, #5, #6, #7 —
  all four are cream-paper Chinese-landscape backdrops. They're
  pretty, but they tell the story of a Chinese restaurant, not
  a kava bar. Re-skinning probably starts by swapping these for
  imagery that says "kava."

No kava imagery anywhere in this set. No brand mark, no
interior photos, no staff. What we have is a generic
Chinese-restaurant template that someone stood up for the
venue and never customized past its defaults — which is
actually good news for a reskin, because there's very little
to preserve.
